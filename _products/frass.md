* We offer a sustainable organic fertilizer and soil amendment made with insect frass and enriched with soil microorganisms. 
* Our organic fertilizer contains essential plant nutrients and organic matter. It enhances the fertility and the physical condition of soils (structure, water infiltration), boosting the growth of plants. 
* Our insect frass fertilizer is a promising alternative to existing mineral and organic fertilizers.
