* We aim to provide a sustainable source of protein for animal and aqua feed, to enhance the nutritional value of farmed animals, and to reduce feed production costs of farmers. 
* Our focus is to offer an affordable, nutritious, and safe insect protein targeted for aqua and animal feed applications. 
* Our insect protein is produced entirely in Benin, utilizing local by-products and waste streams, thereby promoting circular and local economy principles.



# Markets

* Aquafeed
* Poultry feed
* Pig feed
